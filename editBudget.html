Edit Your Budget

To set your goal budget, input your monthly expenditure for each category below. Round up your expenses to the nearest dollar for accuracy.

Use the "Preset Budget" button to quickly select a budget that suits your needs. Simply click on it to apply the preset budget.

Once you've entered your budget and selected a preset if desired, click 'Update Budget' to save your changes. This will allow you to track your expenses and compare them with your target budget.
