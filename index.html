Welcome to BudgetPal!

Thank you for choosing our platform to manage your finances. Here, you can set your goal budget to compare your expenses and make informed financial decisions. Use the navigation bar above to navigate to the other pages in our site or scroll below to learn more about us!

We appreciate your cooperation and wish you success in achieving your financial goals!

How to Use:

You can choose from preset budgets to help you get started. Simply click on the "Edit Budget" button and then the "Preset Budget" button to select a budget that works for you. Or alternatively input your own personalized budget.

Once you've inputted your budget and selected a preset budget if desired, click the 'Update Budget' button located at the bottom of the page to save your changes. Then navigate to the expenses screen by clicking the 'Input Expenses' button on the header. Once you have updated your expenses, click the 'Update Expense' button at the bottom of the screen.

Why BudgetPal?

BudgetPal was created as a project for ComS319 Construction of User Interfaces midterm to provide users with an intuitive tool to manage their finances efficiently. The project is due on March 9th 2024.

Our goal is to simplify budgeting and help users achieve financial stability.
