How to Use

1. Sign up or log in to your BudgetPal account.

2. Input your monthly expenses in the "Expenses" section.

3. Visualize your spending habits and set budget goals.

4. Edit your budget targets as needed to manage your finances effectively.

Why BudgetPal?

BudgetPal was created as a project for COMS 319 midterm to provide users with an intuitive tool to manage their finances efficiently.

Our goal is to simplify budgeting and help users achieve financial stability.
